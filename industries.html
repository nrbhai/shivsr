Cement kiln linings and preheater systems face extreme alkali attack and thermal cycling. Our chamotte provides reliable, long-lasting lining performance.

Applications

  • Cyclone Preheaters
  • Calciner Systems
  • Kiln Inlet/Outlet
  • Cooler Grates

Recommended Grades

  • Shivmotte LF 54
  • Shivmotte LF 60
  • Calcined Bauxite CB 80

Why Shivmotte?

  • Good thermal shock resistance
  • Consistent Al₂O₃ content
  • Competitive pricing

Applications

  • Forehearths
  • Feeder Systems
  • Regenerators

Recommended Grades

  • Shivmotte LF 60
  • Shivmotte LF 70+
  • Calcined Bauxite CB 80+
