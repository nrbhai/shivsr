Testing Methods

How Every Batch is Tested

Our in-house laboratory and established testing protocols ensure consistent chemical and physical properties in every shipment.

🔬

XRF Analysis

X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF) analysis for rapid, highly accurate elemental composition — Al₂O₃, SiO₂, Fe₂O₃, TiO₂ and other oxides verified in every batch.

⚗️

Wet Chemical Analysis

Classical wet chemical analysis used alongside XRF for comprehensive chemical verification, ensuring full compliance with customer and industry specifications.

📐

ASTM C:357 Testing

Industry-standard ASTM C:357 test method for particle size distribution analysis, ensuring accurate gradation reporting for every order dispatched.
