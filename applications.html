Chamotte Applications

From refractory castables and bricks to glass furnace linings and foundry coatings — our chamotte is at the heart of high-temperature industrial processes.

Where Our Material Performs

Shivmotte chamotte and Calcined Bauxite are used across a wide range of refractory and ceramic applications worldwide.

🏗️

Refractory Castables

Chamotte is a key aggregate in refractory castable formulations. Its controlled particle size and consistent chemistry ensure predictable performance at high temperatures.

Refractory Brick Manufacturing

Used as a primary raw material in fire brick production. Superior grain distribution gives consistent green density and final fired properties.

Kiln Furniture

High-duty chamotte used in saggers, kiln shelves, batts and posts. Excellent thermal shock resistance extends component life in repeated firing cycles.

LF 54LF 60LF 70+
⚙️

Foundry Coatings

Chamotte powder (200 Mesh) used in foundry mold coatings and core-making, providing excellent heat resistance and dimensional stability during metal casting.

200 MeshLF 42

Ceramics Bodies

Added to ceramic body formulations to improve mechanical strength, reduce shrinkage, and enhance thermal shock resistance in floor tiles, sanitaryware and technical ceramics.

Glass Furnace Linings

High-alumina chamotte (LF 60/70) and Calcined Bauxite used in glass furnace construction for excellent chemical stability in the presence of molten glass.

Cement Plants
